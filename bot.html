Bot details

A). Martingale Strategy

  • Take Profit: Minimum profit required of total investment made.

  • Name your bot: Set a unique name for your bot so that you can easily search it later.

  • First buy amount (USDT): Amount in USDT purchased for the first time when the bot starts.

B). Martingale Strategy

  • Take Profit: Minimum profit required of total investment made.

  • Type:

    • Oneshot: The bot will stop after selling the investments made. There will be only one cycle of buy & sell.

    • Cycle: The bot will keep buying new quantities after each sell. There will be infinite buy-sell cycles. The bot will stop only after it is forcefully stopped.

C). Martingale Strategy

  • Buy Retracement: The number of positive change in percentage after the total price is less than the drop percentage of VWAP. It is used to identify the uptrend of the market and hence place the new buy order.

  • Sell Retracement: The number of negative change in percentage after the total price is more than the take profit ratio of VWAP. It is used to identify the downtrade of the market and hence place the new sell order. Sell Retracement is not considered during sell orders of sub-position.

D). Martingale Strategy

  • Drop Level Limit: Number of levels upto which purchase of new orders will be made during the drop in the coin price. Every drop will be considered from the VWAP of previous purchase.

  • Configure: Set percentage of drop levels and times the new buy order will be placed with respect to initial buy.

E). Martingale Strategy

  • Drop Level Limit: Number of levels upto which purchase of new orders will be made during the drop in the coin price. Every drop will be considered from the VWAP of previous purchase.

  • Configure: Set percentage of drop levels and times the new buy order will be placed with respect to initial buy.

F). Martingale Strategy

  • Drop level: Number of drop levels

  • Margin Drop Ratio: Minimum percentage drop from a VWAP of previous purchases.

  • Multiple Buy in Ratio: Buy order placed when a drop level is detected. It is times the initial buy set by the user.
